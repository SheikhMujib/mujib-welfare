Our Donate Plan

If someone donates to a specific sector, the Mujib Welfare Foundation spends it in that sector. The general fund is spent on every charitable activity of Mujib Welfare Foundation.

Food for a family

Every year, the Mujib Welfare Foundation carries out relief activities in the flood-hit districts of the country and takes the initiative to stand by the needy and helpless people.

Shelter for a family

Your Zakat has the power to transform lives, from sheltering those who have no home, to feeding families who have endured hunger for days on end.

Education for a child

Educate and taking care of the orphans in the hope of Allah's pleasure and reward is an important act of worship to Allah and it is also one of the best sectors of charity.
